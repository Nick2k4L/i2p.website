The I2P network is currently experiencing instability due to ongoing Denial-of-Service and resource exhaustion attacks.
These attacks are impacting the performance and reliability of I2P services and hidden sites.
The I2P Development Team is actively investigating and implementing mitigations to counter these attacks.
